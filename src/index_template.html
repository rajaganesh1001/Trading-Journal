Strike Finder

Rule-based Nifty option strike-pair calculator from your 3 manual morning inputs — no live pricing, no broker API.
How this works: This is a fast rule-based calculator, not a live option-chain price fetcher. It never contacts NSE or your broker. The 9:07 open is rounded to the nearest 100 for the ATM reference. The first 5-min High is rounded down (descending) to the nearest 100, then reduced by 100 for your first CALL anchor. The first 5-min Low is rounded up (ascending) to the nearest 100, then increased by 100 for your first PUT anchor. Each subsequent row widens the CALL by +100 and the PUT by -100, for 6 rows total.
